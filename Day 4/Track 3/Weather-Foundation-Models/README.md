# Weather Foundation Models - Session Agenda

## Overview
Full-day workshop on weather prediction using AI foundation models, covering data access, forecast generation, evaluation, and skill interpretation.

---

## Schedule

### 8:00 AM – 8:10 AM: Welcome & Orientation
**Duration:** 10 minutes

- Introductions
- Session objectives and structure

### 8:10 AM – 8:20 AM: Computing Environment Overview
**Duration:** 10 minutes

- Introduction to computational resources
- Setup and workflow orientation
- Begin spinning up instance for hands-on activity

### 8:20 AM – 8:30 AM: Background & Context
**Duration:** 10 minutes

- Weather prediction fundamentals
- Role of foundation models in forecasting

### 8:30 AM – 9:10 AM: Hands-on Activity 1 - Data Access & Forecast Generation
**Duration:** 40 minutes

- Reading reanalysis datasets (ERA5, MERRA-2)
- Running medium-range foundation model rollouts

### 9:10 AM – 9:15 AM: Break
**Duration:** 5 minutes

### 9:15 AM – 9:45 AM: Background - Forecast Evaluation
**Duration:** 30 minutes

- Benchmarking approaches for weather and climate
- Overview of forecast skill metrics

### 9:45 AM – 10:25 AM: Hands-on Activity 2 - Evaluation & Visualization
**Duration:** 40 minutes

- Using the statistics/metrics package
- Generating diagnostic plots

### 10:25 AM – 10:30 AM: Break
**Duration:** 5 minutes

### 10:30 AM – 11:00 AM: Discussion - Interpreting Forecast Skill
**Duration:** 30 minutes

- Global vs. regional performance
- Vertical structure and regime-dependent behavior

### 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Open Work Session
**Duration:** 1 hour

- Individual coding time
- 1:1 support
- Small group discussions
